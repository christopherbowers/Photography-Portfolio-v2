Installation instructions:
+++

1. Unzip ledger_mint.zip.

2. Upload the entire /ledger_mint/ folder to your /mint/styles/ folder.

3. Visit Mint in a web browser <http://www.yourdomain.com/mint/> and in the
preferences window change the style to Ledger in the drop down.
